Welcome to the Slovak Community in South Manchester

We are a dedicated team of Volunteers who are committed to unite and empower the Slovak community in South Manchester. We provide education to everyone who wishes to join the Slovak community and our goal is to actively keep cultural traditions alive through various workshops and events.
