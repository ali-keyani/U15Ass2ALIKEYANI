Introduction to the Charity

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum – South Manchester is a registered charity providing Slovak language education, cultural events, and community support to Slovak-speaking families living in the UK.

  • Children and adults learning Slovak
  • Slovak-speaking families returning to or arriving from Slovakia
  • Anyone who values cultural identity and language
